## Full Description

**🐾 Meet Your Perfect Furry Friend**

PetMatchAI uses advanced artificial intelligence to match you with shelter pets that are perfect for your lifestyle. No more endless browsing — our smart algorithm learns your preferences and finds your ideal companion.

**How It Works:**

1. **Take the Lifestyle Quiz** — Answer 10 simple questions about your home, schedule, experience, and preferences

2. **AI-Powered Matching** — Our intelligent algorithm analyzes your answers and matches you with pets from local shelters that fit your lifestyle

3. **Browse Curated Matches** — View detailed pet profiles with compatibility scores, health info, and personality traits

4. **Save Your Favorites** — Build a list of potential matches and revisit them anytime

5. **Connect with Shelters** — Once you find your perfect match, contact the shelter directly through the app

**Features:**

• **Smart AI Matching** — Get matched with pets based on your living space, schedule, budget, and preferences

• **Comprehensive Pet Profiles** — View photos, breed info, personality traits, health records, and adoption fees

• **Advanced Filters** — Filter by species (dogs, cats, birds, small animals), size, age, energy level, and distance

• **Favorites & Lists** — Save pets you love and compare them side-by-side

• **Detailed Pet Information** — Health history, grooming needs, exercise requirements, and estimated monthly costs

• **Shelter Directory** — Browse local shelters and their available pets

• **Dark Mode** — Beautiful dark theme for comfortable browsing

• **Premium Features** — Unlimited matches, advanced AI insights, and ad-free experience

**Premium Subscription:**

Upgrade to Premium for $4.99/month or $39.99/year and unlock:
- Unlimited AI matches
- Advanced compatibility insights
- Ad-free experience
- Priority pet placement notifications

**Privacy & Data:**

Your data stays private. We never share your personal information with shelters without your consent. All pet data comes from partner shelters and rescue organizations.
